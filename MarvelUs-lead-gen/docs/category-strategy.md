# Service Category Strategy for AI Voice Agent Leadgen

## Scoring Methodology
Rank each category on 1–5 across four dimensions:

1. **Appointment/booking dependency** (higher is better)
2. **Phone call volume** (higher is better)
3. **Tech adoption lag** (higher means lower tech adoption = more opportunity)
4. **Average customer value** (higher is better ROI)

**Priority Score = (Booking × 0.35) + (Call Volume × 0.30) + (Tech Lag × 0.20) + (Customer Value × 0.15)**

---

## Tier 1 (Immediate Focus)
These should run daily in Melbourne-first scans.

| Category | Booking | Calls | Tech Lag | Value | Why it matters |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| HVAC | 5 | 5 | 4 | 5 | Emergency demand + high ticket jobs + seasonal spikes |
| Plumbing | 5 | 5 | 4 | 5 | Urgent call-driven jobs and strong repeat/referral economics |
| Roofing | 4 | 4 | 4 | 5 | Fewer but high-value leads; missed calls are expensive |
| Dental | 5 | 4 | 3 | 4 | Appointment-heavy, reception bottlenecks common |
| Chiropractic | 5 | 4 | 3 | 3 | Local clinic scheduling + frequent follow-up calls |
| Med spas | 5 | 4 | 3 | 4 | Booking centric; high margin treatments |
| Law firms | 4 | 5 | 3 | 5 | Inbound calls are highly valuable; response speed impacts close rates |

---

## Tier 2 (Secondary)
Run 2–4x weekly or when Tier 1 density drops.

- Auto repair
- Funeral homes
- Property management
- Cleaning services

Why Tier 2: still call/booking dependent, but mixed margins or higher variability in operational maturity.

---

## Tier 3 (Tertiary)
Run opportunistically for volume or specific campaigns.

- Restaurants
- Retail
- Salons

Why Tier 3: higher competition, thinner margins, and often existing platform tooling; still valid where responsiveness is poor.

---

## Seasonal Planning

- **Summer (Dec–Feb, AU):** HVAC demand spikes; increase HVAC scan frequency and radius.
- **Winter (Jun–Aug, AU):** Heating, plumbing (burst pipes), roofing weather damage checks.
- **Storm periods:** Roofing + emergency trades should be temporarily promoted to highest scan cadence.
- **Tax/financial periods:** Some law practices (tax/business) and compliance-heavy services see call spikes.

Operational rule: shift 20–30% of daily query budget toward categories with active seasonal demand while maintaining baseline Tier 1 coverage.
