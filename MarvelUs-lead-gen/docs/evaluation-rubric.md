# Evaluation Rubric — Penelopi Lead Gate

Use this rubric before surfacing leads to Okeito.

## Surface to Okeito (High-Value Signals)

A lead should be surfaced when one or more of these is true:

- **Must-surface condition:** no chatbot **and** no call button
- **Strong social proof:** Google rating **4.5+** with **50+ reviews**
- **Service match:** category clearly fits AI voice/chat value (call-heavy local services)
- **Legitimacy check:** website appears like a real operating business (not spam/thin affiliate)

## Filter Out (Do Not Escalate)

- Already has a sophisticated chatbot/live-support stack
- Obvious chain/franchise brand (typically slower, harder sales cycles)
- Very low proof of demand (**<5 reviews**)
- Website is broken, parked, or non-existent

## Tie-Breaker Prioritization

If too many qualified leads exist in one day:
1. Prioritize no-chatbot + no-call-button leads first
2. Then prioritize highest lead score / priority score
3. Then prioritize strongest reputation signal (rating + review depth)

## Review Outcome Labels

- **Recommended for outreach** — ready for direct Okeito action
- **Needs manual review** — potentially good but missing one key signal
- **Reject** — low fit or low probability
