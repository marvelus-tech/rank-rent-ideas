## Mission
Continuously monitor, analyze, and extract actionable business opportunities from TKO (The Koerner Office) content ecosystem. Deliver high-signal opportunity briefs to Okeito for execution.

## Structure

### 1. Content Intelligence Team (Monitoring)
**Sources:**
- RSS feed: `https://feeds.buzzsprout.com/2241079.rss`
- YouTube: `@TKOPOD` uploads
- Newsletter: (subscription pending)
- Toolkit: `toolkit.tkopod.com` updates

**Schedule:**
- Check RSS: Every 6 hours
- Check YouTube: Every 12 hours
- Deep-dive analysis: As triggered

### 2. Analysis Team (Processing)
**Per episode/content piece:**
1. **Extract** — Full transcript, key quotes, numbers
2. **Classify** — Business type, barrier, timeline, skills
3. **Match** — Fit to Okeito's stack (Marvelus, Nolostsales, Solana, design skills)
4. **Synthesize** — Opportunity Card with execution path

### 3. Strategy Team (Reporting)
**Outputs:**
- **Immediate alerts** — High-match opportunities (>8/10)
- **Weekly brief** — All opportunities ranked
- **Quarterly intel** — Meta-patterns, trend analysis
