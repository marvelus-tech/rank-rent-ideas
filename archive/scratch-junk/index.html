AI Voice Agent Agency

Score 8 · AI Service · Barrier: $0-500 · Timeline: 2-4 weeks

The Play

Build a white-label AI voice agent service for small businesses. Use Marvelus.cc stack to deploy voice agents that handle inbound calls, qualify leads, and book appointments. Target local service businesses who miss 30%+ of calls.

Why It Works

  • High demand: 67% of SMBs want AI phone answering.
  • Proven model: Similar services charge $200-500 per month.
  • Sticky revenue: Once deployed, hard to switch.
  • Scalable: One agent config can serve many clients.

Okeito's Angle

Leverage existing Marvelus.cc infrastructure. Target Melbourne tradies first, then expand nationally. Differentiator: UX-focused setup and ongoing optimization instead of set-and-forget delivery.

Action Items

  • Research competitor pricing in Melbourne.
  • Build 3 demo voice agents for different verticals.
  • Create landing page with live demo.
  • Reach out to 10 tradies for pilot program.

Resources

  • Marvelus.cc deployment stack
  • SMB call-answering demand benchmark
  • Tradie outreach script and pilot offer template

RV Park Rank & Rent

Score 7 · Real Estate · Barrier: $500-5K · Timeline: 2-3 months

The Play

Build a niche website ranking for “RV parks [city]” or “caravan parks Australia.” Monetize through lead generation or affiliate commissions on bookings.

Why It Works

  • Low competition: Most RV parks have poor websites.
  • High intent: Searchers are ready to book.
  • Recurring revenue: $500-2000 per month per site rental.
  • Asset value compounds as domain authority grows.

Okeito's Angle

Start with Australian caravan parks. Build a comparison and review site with booking affiliate links. Later offer rank-and-rent transfers to owners who want results without managing SEO.

Action Items

  • Run keyword research for “caravan parks [state]” volume.
  • Audit top 10 competitor sites and identify content gaps.
  • Register domain and build MVP in Webflow.
  • Contact 5 caravan parks for partnership pilots.

Resources

  • Webflow for rapid MVP launch
  • SEO keyword research toolset
  • Affiliate booking program shortlists

Niche Marketplace Unbundling

Score 6 · Marketplace · Barrier: $0-500 · Timeline: 3-6 weeks

The Play

Take a category from a big platform (Craigslist, Facebook Marketplace, Gumtree) and build a specialized vertical marketplace with better UX, verified sellers, and category-specific features.

Why It Works

  • Demand is proven because the category already exists.
  • Friction reduction from better UX drives conversion.
  • Network effects: more buyers attract more sellers.
  • Vertical marketplaces have strong acquisition potential.

Okeito's Angle

UX and product background are a direct edge. Start with a category you already understand. Validate with Webflow + Airtable + Stripe before investing in custom engineering.

Action Items

  • Identify 3 underserved marketplace categories.
  • Validate demand via Reddit and Facebook group research.
  • Build no-code MVP with Webflow + Airtable.
  • Get the first 10 listings manually.

Resources

  • Webflow + Airtable + Stripe validation stack
  • Reddit/Facebook demand discovery workflow
  • Manual listing acquisition checklist
