### 2. **Find Motivated Sellers (Your Inventory)**

#### A. Tear-Down Opportunities (Highest Builder Demand)
- **Target:** Old homes on large lots in 78757, 78756, 78759, 78731
- **Signs:** Built 1950–1970, 0.25+ acre, same owner 20+ years, deferred maintenance
- **Sources:**
  - PropStream: Filter — Year built < 1980, Lot size > 0.2 acre, Owner occupied = No
  - Driving for Dollars: 78757 neighborhoods — Crestview, Allandale, Brentwood
  - TCAD: Search by year built + lot size

#### B. Vacant Land Owners (Absentee = Motivated)
- **Target:** Out-of-state owners, inherited land, tax-delinquent
- **Sources:**
  - **BatchLeads/PropStream:** Absentee owner, vacant land, 10+ years owned
  - **Travis County tax delinquent list:** Public record, updated annually
  - **Probate records:** Recently inherited properties

#### C. FSBO / By Owner (No Agent = Flexible Pricing)
- **ByOwner.com** — 520+ Austin land listings by owner
- **Craigslist** — "land for sale by owner" daily
- **Facebook Marketplace** — growing FSBO channel

#### D. Expired / Withdrawn Listings
- **MLS expired:** Land listings that didn't sell = price too high or no traffic
- **Withdrawn:** Seller gave up — call them directly
- **Tools:** PropStream, MLS access (via partner agent)

### 3. **Direct Outreach (Your Engine)**

#### Direct Mail Campaign
- **Target:** 500 absentee vacant lot owners in 78757 + 78756 + 78759
- **Postcard:** "I buy land in Austin. Cash offer in 48 hours. No fees."
- **Cost:** ~$0.50/piece = $250 for 500
- **Response rate:** 1–3% = 5–15 calls
- **Close rate:** 10–20% = 1–3 deals

#### Cold Calling
- **List:** PropStream absentee owners, tax delinquent, probate
- **Script:** 
  > "Hi [Name], I'm [Your Name]. I buy vacant land in Austin. Do you still own the property at [Address]? I'm looking for lots in that area — would you consider selling if the price was right?"
- **Tools:** BatchDialer, Mojo Dialer, REISkip

#### Driving for Dollars
- **Route:** 78757 → 78756 → 78759 → 78731
- **Look for:** Overgrown lots, faded signs, vacant parcels next to new construction
- **Action:** Pin on Google Maps, skip trace owner, send mailer
