## 🎯 THE CORE PROBLEM

**The #1 mistake beginners make:** Picking markets without verifying builder demand.

> "We can lock up deals all day long and if nobody's willing to buy it, they're never going to sell, right?"

**The Solution:** Find where builders are actively pulling permits BEFORE finding sellers.

---

## 🆓 METHOD 1: Free Market Research (Zillow)

### The "Yellow Dot" Technique (Detailed)

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. Go to **Zillow.com**
2. **Switch filters from "For Sale" → "Sold"** (critical — proves actual sales)
3. **Home type:** Select **"Lots/Land" ONLY**
4. **Click "More"** and set:
   - **Sold in last 30 days** (not 90 — this video uses 30 days for faster validation)
   - Apply filters

**What You See:**
- Yellow dots = vacant land sales in the last 30 days
- Clusters = concentrated buyer activity

**The Speaker's Florida Examples:**
| Market | Sales in 30 Days | Sales/Day |
|--------|-----------------|-----------|
| Lehigh Acres | 222 | ~7/day |
| Fort Myers area | High cluster | — |
| Cape Coral | High cluster | — |
| Port Charlotte | High cluster | — |
| Ponta Gorda | High cluster | — |
| Lake Placid | Moderate cluster | — |

**Why This Works:**
> "These yellow dots are sales of vacant land in the area. And then we want to find a market we want to go into."

**The Math He Uses:**
- 222 sales in 30 days = ~7 sales per day
- **"That's what seven sales per day. Over seven sales every single day of vacant land in the last 30 days here."**

**The Limitation:**
> "This could be one-off people buying these properties. This could be someone that's actually going to build their one time off custom home, stuff like that."

Yellow dots show demand, but not necessarily BUILDER demand.

## 🔑 KEY TECHNIQUES & METHODS

### 1. The "30-Day Rule" (vs. 90-Day)
- This video uses 30 days (not 90 like previous video)
- **Why:** Faster validation, more current data
- 30 days = builders are active RIGHT NOW

### 2. The "Builder-First" Market Validation
**Process:**
1. Find builders FIRST (BuyerBridge)
2. Verify they're actively pulling permits
3. THEN find sellers in those markets

**Why this works:**
> "These guys will tell you if it's a deal or not. You can call them right up."

### 3. The "Cluster" Method
**Don't look for individual sales — look for CLUSTERS:**
- 1 yellow dot = maybe a fluke
- 50 yellow dots = a market
- 200+ yellow dots = a HOT market

**The Speaker's Examples:**
- Lehigh Acres: 222 sales = "tons and tons of volume"
- Port Charlotte: 407 sales (from previous video) = even hotter

### 4. The "Spec Builder" Target
**Who to sell to:**
- Spec home builders (not custom one-off builders)
- Building 3+ homes per year
- Buying infill lots (10,000 sq ft rectangular lots)
- **"These little rectangular little 10,000 foot lots"**

**Why spec builders:**
- They buy LOTS of land (volume)
- They have consistent buy boxes
- They close fast (need inventory)
