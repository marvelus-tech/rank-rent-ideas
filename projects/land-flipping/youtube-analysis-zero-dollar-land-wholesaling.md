## 🆓 METHOD 1: The Free Approach (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Find Your Market Using Zillow (Free)

**Process:**
1. Go to **Zillow.com**
2. Switch filters from "For Sale" → **"Sold"**
3. Under "More":
   - Sold in last **90 days**
   - Home type: **Lots/Land only**
4. Scroll around the map looking for **concentrated clusters of yellow dots**
5. These yellow dots = vacant lot sales in the last 3 months
6. **Pick an area with high density** of yellow dots

**Example from video:** Port Charlotte, Florida — 407 vacant lot sales in 90 days

**Why this works:** Yellow dots = proven buyer activity. If builders are buying there, they'll buy from you too.

---

### Step 2: Find Seller Leads Using PropStream (Free Trial)

**Process:**
1. Use PropStream free trial (link in video description)
2. Enter the same market (e.g., Port Charlotte, FL)
3. Apply filters:
   - **Property type:** Vacant land
   - **Status:** Off-market (not listed)
   - **Ownership length:** 10+ years (motivation indicator)
   - **Owner type:** Individual or mixed (not LLC/corporation)
   - **Last sale price:** Under $5,000 (they bought cheap, will sell cheap)

**Result:** ~1,455 qualified leads in Port Charlotte example

**The Math:**
- Properties selling for: $15,000-$20,000
- Your offer: $10,000 + closing costs
- **Profit per deal: $5,000-$10,000**

---

### Step 3: Find Buyers Using Zillow (Free, Manual)

**Process:**
1. Stay in same market on Zillow
2. Switch filters:
   - From "Sold" → **"For Sale"**
   - Home type: **Houses** (not lots)
   - Days on Zillow: **Anytime**
   - Year built: **2025 or newer** (new construction)
3. Results show: **new construction builders actively building**

**Example from video:** 389 new construction houses in Port Charlotte

**Builder names found:**
- Lennar
- Holiday Builders
- Pulte Homes
- LGI Homes
- Dr. Horton
- Custom Homes builders
- Various local builders

**Contact method:**
- Click on listings to get builder/agent contact info
- Call and ask if they want to buy land
- **Note:** You'll deal through agents, which complicates commissions

### Step 4: Lock In Buyer, Then Find Seller

## 🔑 Key Techniques & Methods

### 1. The "Yellow Dot" Market Validation
- Don't guess markets — let data show you
- Zillow sold lots = proven buyer demand
- Concentration = builder activity = your opportunity

### 2. The "10-Year Rule" for Seller Motivation
- Owners who bought 10+ years ago = no emotional attachment
- Bought for under $5,000 = huge margin for you
- Individual owners (not LLCs) = easier to negotiate

### 3. The "Buyer-First" Flip
- Lock builder's buy box BEFORE finding property
- Eliminates risk of unsold inventory
- Know your exact profit margin before making offer

### 4. The "$10K Offer Formula"
- Find lots selling for $15K-$20K
- Offer $10K + closing costs
- Sell to builder at market rate
- Profit: $5K-$10K per deal

### 5. The "New Construction" Buyer Signal
- Year built 2025+ = actively building NOW
- These builders need land NOW
- They're your easiest buyers
