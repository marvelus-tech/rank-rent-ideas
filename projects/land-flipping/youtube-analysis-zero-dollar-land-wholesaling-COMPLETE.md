## 🆓 METHOD 1: THE FREE METHOD (Complete Breakdown)

### PHASE 1: Market Selection (Zillow — Free)

**The "Yellow Dot" Technique in Detail:**

1. Go to **Zillow.com**
2. **Switch filters from "For Sale" → "Sold"** (this is critical — you want PROVEN sales, not listings)
3. Click **"More"** and set:
   - **Sold in last 90 days** (proves recent activity)
   - **Home type: Lots/Land ONLY** (remove houses)
4. **Scroll the map looking for concentrated clusters of yellow dots**
   - Yellow dots = vacant lots that SOLD in the last 90 days
   - **Why 90 days?** Proves builders are actively buying RIGHT NOW
   - **Why clusters?** Builders concentrate in specific areas. One yellow dot = maybe a fluke. 50 yellow dots = a market.

**The Speaker's Examples of Good Clusters:**
- Port Charlotte, Florida: **407 vacant lot sales in 90 days**
- He points out 8-10 different cluster areas on the Florida map
- **His selection criteria:** "Something like this area" (pointing to dense yellow clusters)

**Why This Works:**
> "These are vacant lot sales in the last 3 months. So this is exactly what you want to look for."

The yellow dots are **proof of buyer demand**. If builders bought 407 lots in 90 days, they'll buy yours too.

---

### PHASE 2: Seller Lead Generation (PropStream — Free Trial)

**The Exact Filter Stack:**

After picking Port Charlotte (407 sales), he goes to PropStream and enters the same market. Then he applies these filters IN ORDER:

**Filter 1: Property Type = Vacant Land**
- Removes houses, commercial, etc.
- Result: 96,681 properties → filtered down

**Filter 2: Status = Off-Market**
- "They're not sitting on the market"
- These are people who haven't listed yet but might sell
- **Why:** On-market properties have agents, competition, higher prices

**Filter 3: Ownership Length = 10+ Years**
- "Make sure they've owned it for at least 10 years"
- **Why this matters:**
  - 10+ years = emotional attachment is gone
  - They bought it cheap (likely under $5K)
  - They never built on it = no intention to build
  - They're holding dead money

**Filter 4: Owner Type = Individual or Mixed**
- Removes LLCs, corporations, trusts
- **Why:** Individual owners negotiate easier. LLCs have lawyers, boards, slow decisions.

**Filter 5: Last Sale Price = Under $5,000**
- This is the PROFIT FILTER
- **The logic:** "Every single offer that I'm making to these owners is to an individual or a husband and wife that paid less than five grand for these properties that owned it for at least 10 years."
- **Why under $5K:** They bought it decades ago for pennies. Your $10K offer feels like a fortune.

**Result after all filters:** 1,455 qualified leads

**The Math He Uses:**
- Lots selling for: $15,000-$20,000 (from Zillow yellow dots)
- His offer: $10,000 + closing costs
- Builder pays: $15,000-$20,000
- **His profit: $5,000-$10,000 per deal**

> "So, they have no intention to build on it. It's off-market, so it's not listed on the market. And it's a vacant lot, which is exactly what we're looking for."
