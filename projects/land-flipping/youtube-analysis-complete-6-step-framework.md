## 🎯 THE 6-STEP FRAMEWORK (Speaker's Exact Process)

### STEP 1: Pull a List (PropStream)

**The Process:**
1. Log into PropStream
2. Pick a market (city/county)
3. Apply filters:
   - **Property type:** Vacant land
   - **Status:** Off-market
   - **Ownership length:** 10+ years
   - **Owner type:** Individual
   - **Last sale price:** Under $5,000
4. Export list (typically 500-2,000 leads)

**The Speaker's Commentary:**
> "You can get a free trial for PropStream. The link is in the description."

---

### STEP 2: Get Phone Numbers (Skip Tracing)

**The Process:**
1. Upload PropStream list to skip tracing service
2. Get phone numbers for owners
3. Cost: ~$0.10 per record

**The Speaker's Commentary:**
> "You need to get the phone numbers for these people. You can use BatchSkipTracing, you can use PropStream's built-in skip tracing."

---

### STEP 3: Mass Outreach (Texting/Cold Calling)

**The Process:**
1. Upload numbers to mass texting platform
2. Send 1,000-5,000 texts per day
3. Response rate: 1-3%
4. Cost: $0.03 per text

**The Speaker's Key Insight:**
> "I just reached out to 5,000 in five minutes today. Like, you're not going to be able to compete with somebody that's using systems and softwares like that if you're trying to compete in the same industry, right? You need to be going with the times."

**The Numbers Game:**
- 5,000 texts sent
- 1-3% response = 50-150 responses
- 10-20% of responders = motivated sellers
- 5-10 offers made
- 1-2 deals closed

**The Speaker's Commentary:**
> "If you can reach a million people in a month using what I'm doing and you're reaching 45 people in a month, who's going to make more deals, right? It's just a numbers game."

---

### STEP 4: Negotiate with Sellers (The 60-75% Rule)

**The Process:**
1. Seller responds with interest
2. Offer 60-75% of market value
3. Negotiate to 80-90% of their ask
4. Lock up at price that gives you $5K-$10K margin

**The Exact Conversation Example:**

**Seller:** "I want $40,000 for my land."

**You:** "Sounds good. I'm looking to close quickly and pay cash. Would you do $35,000?"

**Seller:** "No, I need $40,000."

**You:** "I can do $38,000, close in 30 days, pay all closing costs."

**Seller:** "Deal."

**The Math:**
- Market value: $50,000
- Seller wants: $40,000
- You lock up at: $38,000
- Sell to builder at: $45,000
- **Your profit: $7,000**

**The Speaker's Commentary:**
> "You offer them 60 to 75% of market value. That gives you room to mark up the property and sell it to a builder for a profit."

> "Even though I told you they're going to have to pay realtor fees, closing costs, going to take a year on the market. Those aren't selling. So they get an offer at 90% of ask. Either way, they're going to probably walk away with $38 if it's out there for a year. That's what you explain to them."

---

### STEP 5: Send Contract (DocuSign)

**The Process:**
1. Get assignable purchase agreement
2. Fill in: property address, purchase price, closing date (30-45 days)
3. Send via DocuSign to seller
4. Seller signs electronically
5. **You now CONTROL the property without owning it**

**The Speaker's Commentary:**
> "You have it in your DocuSign and then you send it over to their email address or you can send it to their phone number. You fill in the details. The seller will sign it electronically. They don't need to print it or mail it. They can just click sign, print name, finish, and then it'll send back over to you."

> "Now you're in contract. Now you have control of the property without owning it, which is crazy. This contract gives you the right to sell the property to someone else before closing. That's the key to wholesaling."

**The Speaker's Brother's Story:**
> "My brother started this out about two months before I did... Somebody responded and said, 'I don't want to sell my property, but I want to buy lots. I'll buy 20 lots a month. Can you find me some?' And he said, 'Lot? The hell are you talking about lot? What do you mean lot?' And what he did is he called up that guy. That guy ended up being a big builder. He was buying 20 to 30 lots a month, in fact. And he wasn't kidding. My brother found him. The next day, my brother sent a campaign. He locked up, I believe, seven deals at an average spread of about $5,500. He made $38,000 in profit off of one campaign in one day."

---

### STEP 6: Find Builder & Get Paid

**The Process:**
1. Go to Zillow
2. Search for homes built in 2023-2024 within 5 miles of your land
3. Find new construction subdivisions
4. Click on listings, find builder name
5. Google builder + "phone number" or use ChatGPT
6. Call builder: "I have a lot in your area. Do you want to purchase it for $45,000 plus closing?"
7. Builder says yes → send assignment agreement
8. Send both contracts to title company
9. Close in 2-3 weeks
10. **Get paid $7,000 wire/check**

**The Speaker's Commentary:**
> "These big builders have acquisition teams, so don't feel scared. Don't feel like you don't want to reach out to these people. They literally have specific people in place to call and answer and purchase lots. That's literally their full-time job when you're buying 91,000 lots a year."

> "Builders will pay $5 to $10,000 more because it saves them months of searching and negotiating. You're providing speed and convenience."

> "The title company handles all of that stuff with the seller and the buyer. And again, the beauty of this is you just made $7,000 and you never own the land. That's the beauty of this. You didn't use your own money. You didn't take ownership. You simply connected the two parties and got paid for facilitating the transaction."
