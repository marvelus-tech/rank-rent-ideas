## TIER 1 MARKETS — WHERE TO FIND LAND

### 1. TEXAS ⭐⭐⭐ (Top Priority)

**Target Submarkets:**
- **DFW:** Waller, Montgomery, Bastrop, Kaufman counties
- **Houston:** Exurban fringe (no zoning = fast deals)
- **Austin:** Bastrop, Hays, Caldwell counties
- **San Antonio:** Comal, Guadalupe, Medina counties

**Where to Find Off-Market Land:**
| Source | How to Access | What to Look For |
|--------|--------------|------------------|
| **Agricultural land on exurban fringe** | County appraisal district websites, direct mail to farm owners | 5-20 acre parcels, no recent ag activity, aging owners |
| **HBA lot acquisition meetups** | Dallas HBA, Houston HBA, Austin HBA monthly meetings | Builders publish "lot need" criteria publicly |
| **Probate records** | County clerk offices, online probate databases | Estate sales, heirs wanting quick cash |
| **Tax delinquent lists** | County tax assessor websites | Owners behind on taxes = motivated sellers |
| **Water district records** | Municipal utility districts (MUDs) | Land inside/next to MUDs = builder gold |

**Specific Tactics:**
- **Waller County:** Agricultural land transitioning to residential. Look for parcels near Hwy 290.
- **Montgomery County:** Woodlands expansion area. Builders need 0.5-2 acre lots.
- **Bastrop County:** Tesla Gigafactory spillover demand. Look for 1-5 acre parcels.
- **Kaufman County:** DFW sprawl. Target 0.25-1 acre lots for entry-level builders.

**Builder Buy Boxes to Target:**
- **Entry-level:** Lennar, D.R. Horton — 0.15-0.25 acres, $30K-$60K/lot
- **Move-up:** Taylor Morrison, Perry Homes — 0.5-1 acre, $60K-$120K/lot
- **Custom:** Regional builders — 1-5 acres, $100K-$250K/lot
- **Build-to-rent:** AMLI, Greystar — 3-5 acre multi-family parcels

**⚠️ Legal Warning:**
> "Florida wholesaling laws are stricter than most states. You cannot market a property you do not own without a real estate license. You must market the contract (assignment), not the land. Work with a real estate attorney to structure your PSAs and assignment agreements correctly."

**Builder Buy Boxes to Target:**
- **55+ communities:** 0.25-acre, flat, golf-cart-accessible
- **Entry-level SFR:** 0.15-0.5 acre, near employment centers
- **Move-up:** 0.5-2 acres, near good schools

## 📊 THE REVERSE ENGINEERED FORMULA

**Your Market Score =**
```
Builder Ecosystem Depth × Off-Market Land Availability × Assignment Velocity
```

**How to Calculate for Any Market:**
1. **Builder Ecosystem Depth:** Count active builders (3+ permits/year) in target county
2. **Off-Market Land Availability:** Count parcels owned 10+ years, individual owners, agricultural use
3. **Assignment Velocity:** Average days from contract to close (target: 14-30 days)

**Markets to AVOID (Green on Heat Map):**
- 0-20 builders in county
- No major employment drivers
- High property tax + low builder density
- No recent permit activity
