# Builder Finding Prompt Template
## For Buyer-First Land Wholesaling Model

**Based on:** YouTube video method (Carson's Google/ChatGPT approach)  
**Purpose:** Find builder contact info in target markets  
**Cost:** Free (alternative to BuyerBridge.ai)

---

## 🎯 THE MASTER PROMPT TEMPLATE

### Google Search Template

```
[CITY], [STATE], Scattered Lot Home Builders, phone number
```

### ChatGPT Prompt Template

```
I need to find home builders in [CITY], [STATE] who buy scattered lots (not in master-planned communities). 

Please provide:
1. A list of the top 10-20 home builders active in this market
2. Their phone numbers
3. Their websites
4. What types of lots they typically buy (size, price range, location preferences)
5. Any land acquisition or lot purchase contacts if available

Focus on builders who:
- Build single-family homes (not just apartments/townhomes)
- Buy individual lots (not just large developments)
- Are actively building in 2025-2026
- Purchase from wholesalers or individual lot owners
```

## 🔄 THE PROCESS

### Step 1: Run Google Searches
1. Pick 1 market (e.g., "Waller County, Texas")
2. Run Google search: `Waller County, Texas, Scattered Lot Home Builders, phone number`
3. Copy all builder names and phone numbers
4. Repeat for each county in your target market

### Step 2: Run ChatGPT Prompts
1. Use the ChatGPT prompt template above
2. Replace [CITY] and [STATE] with your target
3. Get structured builder list with contact info
4. Save to your CRM

### Step 3: Verify & Enrich
1. Call each builder
2. Confirm they're actively buying lots
3. Get their exact buy box (lot size, price, utilities)
4. Record in CRM

## 💡 PRO TIPS

### 1. The "Scattered Lot" Keyword
**Why it works:**
- "Scattered lot" = individual lots (not master-planned communities)
- These are the builders who buy from wholesalers
- Master-planned community builders = not your target

### 2. The County-Level Search
**Why counties work better than cities:**
- Builders buy by county, not city
- County = tax jurisdiction = how they search
- City searches miss builders who operate across city lines

### 3. The Phone Number Addition
**Why add "phone number":**
- Filters out articles and blog posts
- Gets you directly to contact pages
- Saves time vs. browsing websites

### 4. Alternative Search Terms
If "scattered lot" doesn't work, try:
```
[CITY], [STATE], infill lot home builders, phone number
[CITY], [STATE], individual lot home builders, phone number
[CITY], [STATE], lot acquisition home builders, phone number
[CITY], [STATE], land buying home builders, phone number
[CITY], [STATE], spec home builders, phone number
```
