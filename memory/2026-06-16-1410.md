## **BUSINESS MODEL: Land Wholesaling / Land Flipping (The "Builder Method")**

### **How It Works**
1. **Find vacant land owners** (sellers) who want to sell
2. **Find builders** who want to buy land to build on
3. **Negotiate a purchase contract** with the seller at a price below what the builder will pay
4. **Assign that contract** to the builder for a higher price
5. **Keep the difference** (assignment fee/profit)
6. **Use a title company** to handle the closing

**Key mechanic:** You never own the land. You simply connect seller → buyer and take the spread. The builder pays your fee at closing.

---

### **Revenue Streams**
- **Primary:** Assignment fees (the spread between seller price and builder price)
- **Typical deal size:** $35K average, with "base hits" adding up to big months
- **Big deals:** $85K–$220K+ profit on single transactions
- **Monthly revenue:** $100K–$300K consistently (90% margins)
- **Biggest year:** $2.1M in assignments, $1.7M profit
- **Deal volume:** ~239 land deals/year (nearly 1 per business day at peak)

---

### **Pricing Structure**
- **Seller offer:** Typically $5K–$10K less than what the builder will pay
- **Assignment fee:** Your profit is the spread
- **Example:** Builder pays $35K → You offer seller $28K–$33.5K → You keep $1.5K–$7K
- **Big deals:** Can be 10% of property value (e.g., $750K lot = $75K profit at 10%)

---

### **Target Market**

**Sellers:**
- Out-of-state owners who bought land to build but changed plans
- People who inherited land
- Owners sitting on property taxes for 5+ years
- People who bought multiple lots (with family/friends) — "linked properties"
- Anyone who listed land on Zillow 6+ months ago

**Buyers:**
- **Spec home builders** (builders who build to resell, not custom homes)
- Found on Zillow by filtering for houses built in current year + for sale
- National home builders
- Local builders

**Geographic focus:**
- Started in Lehigh Acres, Florida
- Expanded to 10+ states
- Hot markets with active new construction

---

### **Business Characteristics**
- **90% profit margins** (minimal overhead)
- **No real estate license required**
- **No money down** — you never purchase the land
- **Lumpy revenue** — big deals skew monthly numbers (track quarterly)
- **Not sellable as a business** — no exit potential (unlike SaaS or insurance)
- **High tax burden** — minimal write-offs, big IRS checks at year-end

## **ACTIONABLE STEPS & FRAMEWORKS**

### **The "Zero Money" Startup Playbook**

1. **Generate contracts**
   - Use ChatGPT: "Write me an assignable contract legal in all 50 states for vacant land"
   - Have a lawyer review (or find templates on YouTube)
   - Create buyer contract the same way

2. **Find builders (FREE method)**
   - Go to **Zillow.com**
   - Filter: Houses for sale, built in 2025/2026
   - These are spec home builders (building to resell)
   - Note their name and phone number from the listing
   - Call: *"Hey, you just built 123 Main Street. Do you want more land?"*
   - Get their buy box (what they'll pay, where they want land)

3. **Find sellers (FREE method)**
   - Back on Zillow, click on vacant lots in the same area
   - Look up the owner on **True People Search** (free)
   - Call the owner directly

4. **Make the offer**
   - Offer $5K–$10K less than what the builder will pay
   - Get verbal agreement → send contract

5. **Close the deal**
   - Send both contracts (seller + buyer) to a title company
   - Include all contact information
   - Wait 2–3 weeks
   - Receive wire for the difference

### **The Sales Call Framework**

**Opening:**
> *"Hey [Name], is this the owner of [123 Main Street]? Awesome. I was reaching out — would you by any chance be open to selling this property if I came in at the right price?"*

- Full cash offer
- No realtor fees
- No closing costs (you pay them)
- Net cash to seller
- Close in 2–3 weeks
- Transparency about working with builders
