## **What DCAD Is**

**Dallas County Central Appraisal District** — the county tax assessor's office. They maintain records on **every single property** in Dallas County, including:

- Property address + legal description
- Owner name + mailing address
- Lot size (sq ft)
- **Year built**
- **Land value** vs **improvement value** (building value)
- Tax status (current, delinquent, exempt)
- Property type (residential, vacant, commercial)

DCAD data is **public record** — free to access, no login required for basic searches.

---

## **Why Pre-1980 Teardowns Are the Target**

Your buyer box wants vacant lots or teardowns. Here's the logic:

| Filter | Why It Matters |
|--------|---------------|
| **Built before 1980** | Houses 45+ years old are functionally obsolete. Owners face massive repair costs. |
| **Lot size 8,000+ sq ft** | Your minimum buildable lot size |
| **Improvement value <20% of land value** | The building is worth less than the dirt. Economic teardown. |
| **Owner mailing address ≠ property address** | Absentee owner = higher motivation to sell (out-of-state, inherited, landlord) |
| **Tax delinquent** | Financial stress = motivated seller |

These properties are **NOT on Zillow**. They're not listed. You're buying directly from owners before they ever list.
