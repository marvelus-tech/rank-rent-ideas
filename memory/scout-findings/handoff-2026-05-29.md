**Name:** Eternal Stake Finance
**Ticker:** ESF
**Market Cap:** ~$657K - $1.4M (FDV $1.35M per CoinGecko)
**Category:** DeFi
**Description:** A long-term staking protocol on Solana built around a "Stake Once, Earn Forever" model with capped APY.
**Reward Mechanic:** Users stake ESF tokens with a 2-year lock to earn capped 36.5% APY rewards. Early withdrawal penalties fund the reward pool for remaining stakers.
**Links:**
- Bitrue Overview: https://www.bitrue.com/blog/what-is-esf-eternal-stake-finance-solana

**Confidence:** 65
**Notes:** Extremely small cap. The 2-year lock is a significant commitment. Capped APY provides predictability but also limits upside. Unproven long-term sustainability.
