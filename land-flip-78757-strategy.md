## Lead Sources to Tap

### 1. **FSBO / By Owner Platforms**
- **ByOwner.com** — 520+ Austin land lots listed by owner
  - URL: https://www.byowner.com/austin/texas/property-type=land
  - Filter: For Sale → Land → By Owner (FSBO)
  - These are direct owner contacts, no agent gatekeeping

### 2. **Owner-Financed Land Sellers**
- **Creekside Rural** — 77+ owner-financed TX properties
  - URL: https://www.creeksiderural.com/our-properties/texas
  - Williamson County properties near Austin
  - Some offer owner financing (attractive for flip structure)
- **Liberation Ranches** — Owner-financed land in Texas
  - URL: https://liberationranches.com/owner-financing
  - No credit minimums, $0 down available

### 3. **Distressed / Tax Delinquent**
- **Travis County Tax Delinquent List** — Public record
  - Search: "Travis County tax delinquent properties 2026"
  - Properties with tax liens = motivated sellers
- **Auction.com / RealtyBid** — Foreclosure land auctions
- **HUD / USDA** — Government-owned vacant land

### 4. **Direct Mail / Skip Tracing**
- **Vacant lot owners** in 78757 + surrounding ZIPs (78756, 78758, 78759)
- **Out-of-state owners** — high motivation to sell
- **Inherited properties** — probate leads
- Tools: PropStream, BatchLeads, REISkip

### 5. **Driving for Dollars**
- 78757 is dense/urban — expand to:
  - **Williamson County** (Liberty Hill, Cedar Park, Leander)
  - **Hays County** (Kyle, Buda)
  - **Bastrop County** — cheaper land, growing Austin exurbs
- Look for: overgrown lots, faded for-sale signs, vacant parcels near development

### 6. **Wholesalers & Local Networks**
- **Austin Real Estate Investors Association (REIA)**
- **Local Facebook groups** — "Austin Real Estate Investors", "Central Texas Land Deals"
- **BiggerPockets** — network with active wholesalers
- **Craigslist** — "land for sale by owner" listings (often underpriced)

### 7. **County Records & GIS**
- **Travis County Appraisal District (TCAD)**
  - Search: tcad.org
  - Filter: vacant land, same owner 10+ years, out-of-state mailing address
- **Travis County Clerk** — deed records, recent inheritance transfers
