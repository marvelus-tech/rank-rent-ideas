=== INHOUSE AI EMPLOYEE — Build Prompt ===

Build a productized AI employee service for small businesses.

PRODUCT: InHouseAI — "Hire your first AI employee"

CORE FEATURES:
- Role-specific agents pre-configured for common business functions:
  * Customer Service Agent: handles emails, chat, common questions
  * Sales Assistant: qualifies leads, sends proposals, follows up
  * Admin Agent: schedules meetings, manages calendar, sends reminders
  * Bookkeeping Agent: categorizes expenses, sends invoice reminders
  * Social Media Agent: posts content, responds to comments, tracks mentions
- Simple setup: connect existing tools (Gmail, Slack, Shopify, Square)
- Training: upload company docs, FAQ, past emails — agent learns your business
- Dashboard: see what agent handled today, time saved, tasks completed
- Human handoff: seamless escalation for complex issues
- Pricing: flat monthly fee per agent role (no usage limits)

TECH STACK:
- Agent platform: OpenClaw or custom LangChain setup
- Integrations: Zapier/Make.com for 5000+ app connections
- Knowledge base: Pinecone or Weaviate (vector DB for company docs)
- Voice: Optional ElevenLabs for phone agent
- Dashboard: Next.js + Tailwind
- Backend: Node.js + PostgreSQL
- Auth: Clerk or Auth0

TARGET: Small businesses (1-20 employees), solo operators, tradespeople

PRICING:
- Single agent: $299/month
- Team (3 agents): $799/month
- Unlimited: $1499/month
- Setup fee: $500 (one-time training)

SUCCESS CRITERIA:
- Business owner saves 15+ hours/week within 30 days
- Agent handles 80%+ of routine inquiries without human
- Customer satisfaction unchanged or improved
- ROI positive within first month (time saved > cost)
