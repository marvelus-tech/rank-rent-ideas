=== NICHE INDEED JOB BOARD — Build Prompt ===

Build a specialized job board for ONE profession.

PRODUCT: NurseJobz (or SolarTechJobs, etc.) — "The only job board that matters"

CORE FEATURES:
- Scrape Indeed via Apify for one niche: e.g., "Nurse Practitioner"
- Clean listings: title, hospital/clinic, location, salary (if shown), requirements
- Filter by: state, salary range, experience, specialty
- Job alerts: email daily/weekly with new matches
- Employer profiles: photos, ratings, culture info
- Resume upload: candidates create profile
- Employer posting: $199/post (vs Indeed's $500+)
- Mobile-first design (most job seekers are mobile)

TECH STACK:
- Frontend: Next.js + Tailwind
- Backend: Node.js + Express
- Scraper: Apify Indeed scraper
- Database: PostgreSQL
- Email: SendGrid
- Payments: Stripe (job posting fees)
- Hosting: Vercel + Railway

TARGET: Job seekers in specific professions, niche employers, recruiting agencies

SUCCESS CRITERIA:
- 50+ active jobs within 30 days of launch
- Daily email alerts sent to 100+ subscribers
- First employer pays to post within 60 days
