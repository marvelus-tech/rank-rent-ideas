=== AI AUDIT FRAMEWORK — Build Prompt ===

Build a service that sells AI readiness assessments to small businesses, then upsells implementation.

PRODUCT: AI Audit Pro — "Find $10K+ in wasted time in 20 minutes"

CORE FEATURES:
- Voice agent "Annie" conducts 20-30 min phone interviews with business owners
- Automated report generation: identifies top 5 time-wasting processes
- Report includes: process name, hours wasted weekly, AI solution, estimated savings, implementation difficulty
- Upsell path: $1,000 audit → $3-5K implementation → $500/month maintenance
- White-label option for consultants/agencies

TECH STACK:
- Voice agent: Vapi.ai or Bland AI for phone calls
- Transcription: Whisper (OpenAI)
- Analysis: Claude/GPT-4 for report generation
- Report delivery: PDF generation + email
- CRM: Airtable or Notion for tracking leads
- Scheduling: Calendly integration

THE INTERVIEW FLOW:
1. "What's your name and what do you do?"
2. "How long have you been in business?"
3. "Solo or team? How big?"
4. "What software do you use day-to-day?"
5. "What's the biggest headache in your workday?"
6. "If you could wave a magic wand, what would you automate first?"
7. AI analyzes responses + generates prioritized roadmap

TARGET: Small business owners (5-50 employees), solo operators drowning in admin

SUCCESS CRITERIA:
- 10 audits completed in first month
- 30% conversion to implementation ($3-5K)
- Average client saves 15+ hours/week
