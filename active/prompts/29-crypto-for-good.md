=== CRYPTO FOR GOOD NONPROFITS — Build Prompt ===

Build a platform that connects crypto donors with nonprofits for tax-efficient giving.

PRODUCT: CryptoGive — "Donate crypto, maximize impact"

CORE FEATURES:
- Donor portal: donate 100+ cryptocurrencies with one click
- Auto-conversion: donations auto-swap to stablecoins (USDC) for nonprofits
- Tax receipts: auto-generated PDF receipts with fair market value at time of donation
- Nonprofit dashboard: track donations, donor analytics, campaign management
- Yield farming for good: donor's crypto earns yield while held, proceeds go to charity
- NFT charity mints: limited edition NFTs where proceeds go to causes
- Recurring donations: set up automated monthly crypto giving
- Matching campaigns: corporate matching integrated

TECH STACK:
- Blockchain: Solana (low fees, fast) + Ethereum (broad support)
- Wallet integration: Phantom, MetaMask, WalletConnect
- Swaps: Jupiter (Solana), Uniswap (ETH) for auto-conversion
- Payments: Coinbase Commerce or custom checkout
- Backend: Node.js + PostgreSQL
- Frontend: Next.js + Tailwind
- Tax docs: PDF generation + email delivery
- KYC/Compliance: SumSub or Onfido for nonprofit verification

TARGET: Crypto holders (tax-efficient giving), nonprofits (new revenue stream), DAOs (treasury diversification)

PRICING:
- Free for donors (network fees only)
- 1-2% fee on donations to nonprofits (vs 5-10% traditional)
- Premium features: $99/month for nonprofits (analytics, CRM, campaigns)

SUCCESS CRITERIA:
- 10+ nonprofits onboarded in first month
- $50K+ in donations processed in first 90 days
- Average donation size >$500 (crypto donors give more)
