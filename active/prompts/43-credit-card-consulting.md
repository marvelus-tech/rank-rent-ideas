# Build Prompt — Credit Card Consulting Business

## Context
You are building a business replicating Colin Stroud's Go Somewhere model: a credit card rewards consulting service for business owners. This is a proven $231K/year solo business model with 83% profit margins.

## Goal
Launch a credit card consulting side hustle that generates $1,000/month within 90 days, following the exact playbook Colin used.

## Your Role
You are the founder. You will learn credit card points strategy, create content on LinkedIn, and sell $400-875 consulting packages to business owners.

## Phase 1: Education (Week 1-2)

### Learn the Domain
1. Read the following free resources:
   - The Daily Drop newsletter + free course on their website (best starting point per Colin)
   - Frequent Miler
   - The Points Guy
   - One Mile at a Time

2. Use AI (Claude/ChatGPT) as your study partner:
   - "Explain how credit card point transfers work"
   - "Compare Chase, Amex, Capital One business cards for someone spending $30K/month on ads"
   - "How do I book business class to Europe using points?"

3. Practice by researching real trips:
   - Pick a destination (e.g., Hawaii, Europe, Japan)
   - Find the best point deals using Seats.aero
   - Get all the way to booking stage without actually paying
   - Document what you learned

### Key Concepts to Master
- **Earn and burn** — Points devalue faster than cash. Don't hoard.
- **Transfer partners** — Booking through card portals = 1¢/point. Transferring to airlines/hotels = 2-5¢/point.
- **Earning rates matter more than perks** — A $0 annual fee card with 2x earning beats an $895 Platinum with 1x earning for high spenders.
- **Business credit cards** — Most business owners use 1% cashback local bank cards and have no idea they could earn 2-4x points.

## Phase 2: Validation Content (Week 2-4)

### The "Learn in Public" Strategy
Before you have clients, post about what you're learning.

**Content formula:**
1. Research a deal for 3-5 hours
2. Find something interesting (even if you don't book it)
3. Post: "I just spent 5 hours researching how to fly to [X] in business class for [Y] points. Here's what I learned..."
4. Share the mechanics, the gotchas, the real value

**Example topics for posts:**
- "Business owner spending $40K/month on a 1% cashback card? You're leaving $12,000/year on the table."
- "I found a way to get to Hawaii for 7,500 points. Here's the exact steps."
- "Chase vs Amex vs Capital One for construction company owners — the math"
- "Why your credit card 'perks' are costing you more than they're worth"

### Platform: LinkedIn
- Post 1-2x per day, 6 days a week
- Target: Business owners, not travel hackers
- Tone: Helpful, direct, "I just learned this and it's crazy"
- No outbound DMs — let them come to you

### First Viral Post Goal
Find one interesting deal, do the legwork, post the discovery. If it gets 10K+ impressions and 5+ DMs, you have validation.

## Phase 3: Productize & Price (Week 4-6)

### Offer Structure

**Option A: The Colin Model — $875**
- 20-min discovery call
- 45-min research/prep
- 75-min live consult call
- Written strategy document
- Email/text implementation support
- One trip planning session (included)

**Option B: Starter Model — $400-500**
- Same structure, just lower price for first 5 clients
- Raise price by $100 after every 2 clients until you hit $875
- OR: Remove trip planning, make it strategy-only

### Positioning
- "I help business owners stop leaving money on the table with their credit card spending"
- NOT "credit card hacking" or "churning" — banks hate those terms
- Use "strategy," "optimization," "points consulting"

### Client Criteria
- Business owners spending $20,000+/month on credit cards
- Blue-collar businesses: construction, medical, trades, manufacturing, logistics
- NOT tech/finance people who think they already know points
- They should feel like they're "not getting enough out of their spending"

## Phase 4: Operations (Week 6-12)

### The Consult Call Script

**Discovery (20 min):**
1. What business are you in?
2. How much do you spend per month on credit cards? (personal + business)
3. What cards do you currently have?
4. What are your travel goals? (if any — some clients never travel)
5. What's your biggest frustration with your current setup?

**Research (45 min — offline):**
1. Map their spending categories (ads, inventory, materials, travel, etc.)
2. Identify optimal card mix (Chase, Amex, Capital One — usually 1-2 cards max)
3. Calculate current earning vs. optimized earning
4. Find 1-2 specific high-value redemption options

**Delivery (75 min — live call):**
1. Show them the math: "You're earning X now. With this strategy, you'd earn Y."
2. Recommend specific cards with reasoning
3. Explain signup bonuses (if applicable) and ongoing earning
4. Walk through one transfer partner example
5. Set expectation for trip planning follow-up

**Follow-Up (30 min — written):**
1. Send detailed strategy doc with exact card names, application links (affiliate), timeline
2. Include "next 3 trips you could take with your projected points"
3. Offer email/text support for implementation

## Phase 5: Scale (Month 4-6)

### Trip Research Service
Once you have more demand than you can handle:
1. Hire points experts (find on Reddit r/awardtravel, FlyerTalk, or train existing clients)
2. Charge $50 deposit + $200-500 based on scope
3. Build simple CRM in Notion/Airtable to track active trips
4. Your margin: 30-50% after paying researcher

### Content Flywheel
- Post client success stories (with permission)
- "Just helped a construction company owner earn 800K points this year — here's how"
- Screenshots of booking confirmations (redact personal info)
- Before/after earning rate comparisons

### Email List
- Offer free guide: "The 3 Cards Every Business Owner Should Have"
- Weekly newsletter: 1 deal highlight + 1 strategy tip + 1 client story
- Monetize: Affiliate links + promote your consulting

### Price Escalation
- First 3 clients: $400
- Next 3: $500
- Next 3: $650
- Then: $875 (Colin's current price)
- Test higher: $1,000-1,500 for clients spending $100K+/month

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Post consistently** — 1-2x/day on LinkedIn is non-negotiable for first 6 months
2. **Target the right people** — Blue-collar business owners, not travel nerds
3. **Do the math** — Always show ROI. "You'll earn 400K more points = $4,000+ in value"
4. **Don't overcomplicate** — Most clients need 1-2 cards. Don't sell them on 7-card "churning" strategies.
5. **Be human** — AI can research. You can empathize, guide, and reassure busy business owners.

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

- **Underpricing** — $875 sounds like a lot but the ROI is 10x for the right client
- **Over-delivering on trips** — Some trip planning takes 4+ hours. Set expectations.
- **Chasing premium cards** — A $0-fee 2x card often beats a $895 Platinum for pure earners
- **Hoarding points mindset** — Teach clients to earn and burn
- **AI over-reliance** — AI gives best-case scenarios. Real deals require human verification
- **Calling it "hacking"** — Banks will reject affiliate applications

## Adaptations for Your Market

If you're not in the US:
- Research your country's credit card ecosystem (Australia has different programs)
- Points programs vary by region (Amex MR, Chase UR, Capital One don't exist everywhere)
- The core model still works: help business owners optimize spending → earn more → redeem better
- Research: What are the best business credit cards in [your country]?

Execute this prompt by:

3. Identifying your first target niche (e.g., "plumbers in Melbourne spending $25K/month")
4. Writing your first 5 LinkedIn post drafts
5. Setting up your basic tools (Calendly, Stripe, Beehiiv)
