=== DIGITAL LEGACY AGENT — Build Prompt ===

Build an AI that interviews people and creates interactive digital twins to preserve their legacy.

PRODUCT: LegacyAI — "Talk to your loved ones, forever"

CORE FEATURES:
- Interview mode: AI asks questions about life, values, stories, wisdom
- Voice capture: records and clones voice using ElevenLabs
- Photo integration: uploads photos, AI narrates the stories behind them
- Interactive twin: family can "talk" to the digital version after passing
- Memory vault: searchable archive of stories, recipes, advice, life lessons
- Ethical controls: consent at every stage, opt-out anytime, data deletion
- Family sharing: invite family members to contribute memories
- Timeline view: life events mapped chronologically with media

TECH STACK:
- Interview AI: Claude/GPT-4 with empathy-tuned prompts
- Voice cloning: ElevenLabs Voice Cloning API
- Speech-to-text: Whisper API
- Storage: AWS S3 or Cloudflare R2 for media
- Database: PostgreSQL (interview data, relationships)
- Frontend: Next.js + Tailwind (calming, respectful design)
- Security: End-to-end encryption for sensitive content

TARGET: Families with aging relatives, terminally ill patients, estate planners, hospices

PRICING:
- One-time setup: $299-499 (interview + twin creation)
- Storage: $9/month for ongoing access
- Hospice partnership: bulk pricing for facilities

SUCCESS CRITERIA:
- Complete interview in 3-5 sessions (30 min each)
- Voice clone is recognizable to family
- Family interacts with twin 5+ times in first month
- 0 data breaches or consent violations
