## Why This Matters for Okeito

### Threat Assessment

1. **Competitors will outpace manual outreach**: If Marvelus.cc / Nolostsales.cc rely on human SDRs or basic automation, competitors using AI SDRs will generate 2-3x more pipeline at lower cost.
2. **Speed of adoption**: 80% of enterprise apps expected to embed agents by 2026. The window to adapt is closing.
3. **Vertical specialization**: AI SDRs are emerging for specific industries (healthcare, financial services, manufacturing). SMB-focused tools will follow.

### Opportunity Assessment

1. **Integration play**: Position Marvelus/Nolostsales as the **data source** or **enrichment layer** for AI SDRs. Cleanlist's waterfall enrichment model (15+ providers, 98% accuracy) proves the value.
2. **White-label AI SDR**: Build or partner to offer autonomous outreach as a feature, not just lead lists.
3. **Signal-based targeting**: If Marvelus has proprietary data (intent signals, technographics), AI SDRs are the perfect distribution channel.
